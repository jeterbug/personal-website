About Me

Strategy meets execution.

I'm Jeter Cotton, a Director of Project Management based in Texas with a rare blend of creative instinct and operational precision. I've led cross-functional teams across digital product launches, design system builds, and strategic campaigns — bringing clarity to ambiguity and momentum to every initiative.

My background in design and technology means I don't just manage timelines — I understand the craft behind the work. I lead teams that care about quality, move with purpose, and consistently deliver beyond expectations.

Whether I'm aligning stakeholders, unblocking a sprint, or architecting a workflow from scratch, my goal is always the same: make the complex feel simple, and the impossible feel inevitable.
